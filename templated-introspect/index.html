Welcome to Scheff's Kitchens & Cabinets

Established since 1996 and with over Twenty Years Experience.

Designed and built with quality in mind and with more than two decades experience, Scheff's Kitchens & Cabinets has created functional, beautiful and unique kitchens and cabinets. Through consultation and a free in-house inspection, we can custom build your kitchen and cabinets to suit your individual needs and lifestyle.

Scheff's Kitchens & Cabinets can also arrange the plumbing, tiling, splashbacks and any electrical work needed to complete your job, if required. Renovate with ease. Let us take care of organising it all for you.

So for friendly personalised serviced, high quality workmanship and units made to your specifications, you cant go past Scheff's Kitchens and Cabinets. I also have a small showroom which you are all welcome to come and take a look at by appointment only.
